ARTERIAL STUDY RIGHT LOWER EXTREMITY


PROCEDURE:

Lower extremity duplex imaging was performed with high frequency linear array transducer.

Duplex examination included imaging in the transverse, sagittal planes with both grayscale and color Doppler.

Pulsed wave spectral Doppler data was acquired for common femoral, superficial femoral (proximal, middle, distal), deep femoral, popliteal, anterior tibial and posterior tibial arteries.


FINDINGS:

No atherosclerotic disease in lower limb arteries.

These vessels showed adequate perfusion on color Doppler.

Spectral analysis showed normal velocities and triphasic flow in all the arteries.

No Baker's cyst.


IMPRESSION:

Normal study.
