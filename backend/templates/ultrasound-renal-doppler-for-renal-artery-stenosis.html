renal doppler for renal artery stenosis


clinical info: hypertension.

technique:

scan was performed with low frequency curvilinear transducer. gray scale and color flow mapping and spectral analysis were performed.

right kidney:

right kidney measures 10.6 cm in bipolar length. renal parenchymal echogenicity is normal. minimal right hydronephrosis is seen.

evaluation of main renal artery shows peak systolic flow of 84 cm/sec. peak systolic velocity in the aorta is 85 cm/sec. renal artery/aorta peak systolic ratio of 0.94 is noted.

doppler evaluation of main renal vein shows continuous flow with normal respiratory variation.

normal flow is seen in right kidney.

spectral waveform take at inter, upper and upper pole vessels. the ri are essentially normal.

doppler evaluation of inter-lobar were obtained and ri were calculated.

at upper pole ri = 0.58

at interpolar region ri = 0.6

at lower pole ri = 0.59
