doppler carotids


technique:

carotid triplex imaging was performed with high frequency linear array transducer. pulsed wave and spectral data were also acquired bilaterally.

findings:

right carotids & vertebral:

grayscale images show calcified plaque in right bulb extending into proximal ica resulting in approx. 43 % luminal narrowing. wave patterns are normal. right vertebral artery showed antegrade continuous flow. imt= 0.7 mm

left carotids & vertebral:

grayscale images show calcified plaque in left bulb extending into proximal ica resulting in approx. 57% % luminal narrowing. wave patterns are normal. left vertebral artery showed antegrade continuous flow. imt= 0.8 mm
