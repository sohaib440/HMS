venous study of upper limbs


procedure & findings:

venous system of both upper limbs was evaluated with high frequency, linear array transducer. grayscale, color flow and doppler spectral analysis was done supplemented by compression and augmentation maneuvers.

bilateral subclavian, axillary, brachial, ulnar and radial veins are compressible and showed augmentation on distal compression.

evaluation of superficial venous system also reveals adequate flow.

color doppler showed adequate filling of vessels.

no evidence of intra luminal thrombus in either veins is noted.

no collateral circulation could be recognized.


impression

- no dvt.

- patent arteries.

- excessive subcutaneous fat.
